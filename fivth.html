About Us

Welcome to HJ Sports

Established in October of 2002, Huijun Sports has been in the business for 15 years integrated with commerce, industry and trade. We apply scaled operation and standard management. The headquarter is located in Yiwu International Business Center with 7 direct-sale stores in the Yiwu International Trade City, Yiwu Huijun Sports Co., Ltd, Zhejiang Huijun Import & Export Co., Ltd, Yiwu Jiandao Fitness Facilities Co., Ltd and Northern China Operation Center. The joint production plants are located in Zhejiang, Fujian, Hebei and Shandong provinces.
